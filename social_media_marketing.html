Social Media Marketing and Digital Advertising

Social media marketing is a process of gaining traffic by promoting a product or a service through social media websites. it uses platforms that allow people to exchange any kind of information. the most popular social media platforms are: facebook, youtube, twitter, linkedin, pinterest and tumblr. each of these social media networks has its own tools that can be used for different purposes of marketing. social media allows companies to interact with their audiences through instant chats, fan pages, interactive content. due to the inbuilt analytical tools it is possible to later analyze the involvement and engagement of the audience with a specific company or brand.

Digital Advertising

Digital (or online) advertising is a form of marketing and advertising which uses the Internet to deliver promotional marketing messages to consumers in a form of banners or other ad formats like text, images, flash, video, and audio. In other words, it is a way to pitch your target audience on each step of their customer journey and convince it to head their buying intentions toward your brand.
